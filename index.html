ABOUT OUR ORGANIZATION

Our organization aims to spread awareness on the dangers of gun violence, specifically in men aged 16-26. We strive to create a safer environment where parents are not afraid to let their children roam free. The mortality rate is high, and the consequences of gun violence do not allow for second chances; however, this does not have to be the case. Together, we can put an end to it.

GUN RELATED DEATHS/INJURIES.

Injuries and fatalities from firearms remain a major public health concern in the US. All communities are impacted by firearm violence and injuries, although some groups are more likely than others to sustain a firearm injury. Whole communities' perception of safety and security can be impacted by shooting occurrences, whether they take place in homes, schools, places of worship, businesses, retail establishments, on the street, or at communal gatherings. These tragedies can also influence daily decisions.

SUICIDES

Suicide is defined as death caused by self-directed injurious behavior with intent to die because of the behavior. Firearm suicide rates are at record high levels. With such easy access to firearms, suicides are becoming more impulsive than ever. Firearm suicides have a devastating impact on the safety and well-being of Americans.

OUR BLUEPRINT

Here is what happens when you donate to these organizations

Impact 1

Sandy Hook Promise

Your support directly funds Know the Signs programs, training students and educators to recognize early warning signs of chronic social isolation or potential school crises before violence happens.

Impact 2

Safer TN

Your donation helps advance evidence-based firearm safety policies locally. They work to bridge gaps between gun owners and non-owners to push forward practical, lifesaving Tennessee laws.

Impact 3

Giffords

Funds community violence intervention programs across the country. They fight the crisis from all legal angles, holding the gun lobby accountable and defending commonsense state statutes.
